Vocabulary Story Guidelines

This page provides guidelines, policies, and rules for people who create and review vocabulary stories.

General Guidelines

  • Keep each story focused on clear vocabulary learning goals.
  • Use age-appropriate, respectful, and inclusive language.
  • Write in simple, natural sentences with correct grammar and punctuation.
  • Ensure each target word appears in meaningful context.

Policies for Content Creators

  • Define the target vocabulary list before drafting the story.
  • Avoid offensive, discriminatory, or unsafe content.
  • Do not copy copyrighted text; write original stories.
  • Include variety in examples so learners understand word usage in different contexts.

Rules for Reviewers

  • Verify that the story uses the target vocabulary accurately.
  • Check clarity, flow, and reading difficulty for the intended audience.
  • Flag factual errors, ambiguity, and any policy violations.
  • Provide specific, constructive feedback with clear revision requests.
